Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator

The test performed is Body Mass Index (BMI), which is calculated from body Weight (W) and height(H). BMI = W / (H x H), where W = body weight in kilograms and H = height in meters. The higher the score usually indicates higher levels of body fat.
